About

Promoting healthy lifestyle and supporting environmental protection the Aparg team presents a free, unique application for bike lovers.

Velopark is an application developed for those who consider bike riding a lifestyle or just a hobby. It allows one to detect the nearest bike parking spots, rent spots, maintenance centers, shops and spare parts sellers centers.

One of the Velopark's advantages is a user friendly interface. The social nature of the app allows users community members to add new places and validate information.

Start riding with Velopark!

FAQ

How to use home screen?

Open Velopark app on your phone.

Press NAVIGATE button at the right lower corner to find your position on the map.

Map shows you different spots represented by balloons. Click the balloon about which you would like to find out more information, and you will see the image and other details about it. To see the image in a closer way just click on it.

You can change type of viewing spots. Go to the right upper corner and push the ARROW button. It will drop down with 4 types of spots: parking, rent, shop and spare parts. Choose one or several types of spots which you would like to view. Each type of spot is represented in different colors so it is easy to get oriented among so many spots.

Left upper corner button represents MENU which suggests you to add or review spots.

How to add spots?

Open Velopark app on your phone.

In the left upper corner you will see MENU button. Press menu button, it will show drop down menu. Hit ADD button.

Application will suggest you 2 option for uploading image for the coming spot: either to select already existing image from the GALLERY by pushing gallery button and selecting the right image or to take a picture pushing CAMERA button.

Then fill “Name” cell adding spot's name and check if the automatically filled in address is correct. If the address is not correct, or you have selected image from the Gallery fill in the address manually and put the landmark on the map at the right place.

Then scroll down and select the right type for the spot: P for parking, R for rent, or appropriate sign for shops or spare parts and repair.

Click SUBMIT. Voila! You are done !

After validation by community members your spot will be shown on the map.

How to review spots?

All spots which are added by users need to be reviewed for an accuracy reasons. If there is a spot which needs to be reviewed the app will show you a notification on the left upper corner.

Push MENU button, then in a drop down menu hit REVIEW button. Choose an option vote for or against if the spot contains inappropriate message or incorrect information.

After validation by community members your spot will be shown on the map.

How to ask to remove spots?

If you noticed that some spots changed their location or doesn't exist any more you can suggest to delete them.

Click the balloon about which you want to delete and after that click on ASK TO REMOVE button under image.

After validation by community members your spot will be deleted on the map.
