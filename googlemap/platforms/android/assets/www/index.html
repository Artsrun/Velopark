About

Promoting healthy lifestyle and supporting environmental protection Aparg team presents free and unique application for bike lovers in Armenia.

Velopark is an application developed for those who consider bike riding as a lifestyle or just a hobby. It allows to detect the nearest bike parking spots in Armenia. Velopark makes also possible to view the nearest bicycle rent spots, bike maintenance centers, shops and spare parts sale centers.

One of the Velopark's advantages is user friendly interface. Social nature of the app provides an opportunity to add new places with detailed information after being validated by community members.

The application is available for both iOS and Android mobile platforms.
