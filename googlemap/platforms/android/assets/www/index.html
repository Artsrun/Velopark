About

Promoting healthy lifestyle and supporting environmental protection Aparg team presents free and unique application for bike lovers in Armenia. Velopark is an application developed for those who consider bike riding as a lifestyle or just a hobby. It allows to detect the nearest bike parking spots in Yerevan and partly in regions of Armenia. Velopark makes also possible to view the nearest bicycle rent spots, bike maintenance centers, shops and spare parts sale centers. One of the advantages of the app is its friendly interface. Due to its social component it also provides an opportunity for adding new places with detailed information such as address, coordinates, pictures by community members after being validated by other members. The application is available for both Ios and Android mobile platforms. Start riding with Velopark!
